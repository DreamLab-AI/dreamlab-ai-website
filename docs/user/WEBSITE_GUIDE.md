# DreamLab AI Website User Guide

Welcome to DreamLab AI. This guide will help you navigate the website and find the training and consulting services that match your needs.

## Getting Around the Website

### Main Navigation

The website header provides quick access to all sections:

- **Home** - Landing page with overview of services
- **Team** - Meet our 44+ specialists and instructors
- **Work** - View our portfolio of completed projects
- **Workshops** - Browse available training programs
- **Contact** - Get in touch with us

### Key Pages

#### Home Page
The landing page introduces DreamLab AI and features:
- Overview of AI agent training and consulting
- Quick navigation to main services

- Email signup for updates

#### Team Page

1. Browse profiles of over 44 team members

#### Work Page

- Case studies with descriptions

#### Workshops Page

## Services Overview

### Corporate Workshops
**Duration:** 1 day | **Participants:** up to 4 | **Location:** Your site or ours

Perfect for teams wanting hands-on training. Workshops include:
- Development environment setup
- Version control and Git
- AI API integration
- Building working agents
- Real-world applications

**How to Book:**
1. Browse workshops on the Workshops page
2. Click on a workshop for full details
3. Review the curriculum and schedule
4. Click "Enquire" or use the Contact form
5. A team member will reach out to confirm dates

### Residential Training
**Duration:** 2-5 days | **Participants:** Up to 4 | **Location:** Full-board accommodation

Immersive training programs including:
- AI Commander Week (5 days, £2,750 per person)
- Virtual Production Master (5 days, £2,950 per person)
- Sovereign AI Systems (3 days, specialized focus)
- Full-board accommodation with meals included
- Small group size for personalized attention
- 15% discount for groups of 4

**Features:**
- Expert instructors with real-world experience
- Hands-on projects and exercises
- One-on-one support throughout
- Certificate of completion
- Ongoing access to materials

**How to Book:**
1. Visit the Residential Training page
2. Review program details and curriculum
3. Check dates and availability
4. Click "Book Now" or contact us via the form
5. Confirm dates and complete the booking process

### Bespoke Consulting
Custom AI implementation and strategy work:
- Tailored to your organization's needs
- Multi-day or ongoing engagement
- Can be combined with training
- Flexible format (remote, on-site, or hybrid)
