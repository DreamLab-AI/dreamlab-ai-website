# How to Book a Workshop

This guide walks you through booking a DreamLab AI workshop for your team.

## Before You Start

Before booking, have ready:
- Your team size (minimum 1, maximum 6 people)
- Preferred dates and duration
- Your location or preferred training venue
- Workshop topic of interest
- Contact information (email, phone)

## Step-by-Step Booking Process

### Step 1: Browse Available Workshops

1. Go to the **Workshops** page
2. View the complete list of available training sessions
3. Each workshop includes:
   - Workshop title and code
   - Duration (typically 3 hours for morning/afternoon sessions)
   - Difficulty level (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced)
   - Topic focus area
   - Learning objectives

### Step 2: Review Workshop Details

Click on any workshop to see:
- **Full Curriculum** - Detailed learning modules
- **Prerequisites** - Any required prior knowledge
- **Learning Outcomes** - What you'll be able to do after
- **Format** - Whether it's hands-on coding, theory, or mixed
- **Next Steps Links** - Related workshops to take after

### Step 3: Check Your Needs

Confirm the workshop matches your team:
- Does the difficulty level suit your experience?
- Do you have the prerequisites covered?
- Is the duration compatible with your schedule?
- Will the learning outcomes help your team?

### Step 4: Submit Your Inquiry

Option A - Via the Workshop Page:
1. Click the **"Enquire"** button on the workshop
2. You'll be taken to the contact form
3. The workshop will be pre-selected

Option B - Via the Contact Form:
1. Go to the **Contact** page
2. Select **"Workshop Booking"** as your inquiry type
3. Specify which workshop(s) you're interested in
4. Provide your team information

### Step 5: Fill Out the Contact Form

Complete the following fields:

**Your Information:**
- **Name** - Your full name
- **Email** - Your email address (we'll use this to contact you)
- **Organization** - Your company name (optional)

**Workshop Details:**
- **Workshop Selection** - Choose from available workshops
- **Team Size** - How many people from your team will attend (1-6)
- **Preferred Dates** - Your availability (suggested dates or date ranges)
- **Location** - Where you'd like the workshop delivered
  - Your offices
  - DreamLab offices
  - Hybrid/remote
  - Other location

**Additional Information:**
- **Team Member Preferences** - Any specific instructors you'd like
- **Special Requirements** - Accessibility needs, dietary restrictions, etc.
- **Additional Notes** - Anything else we should know about your team

### Step 6: Team Selection (Optional)

When submitting your inquiry:
1. Go to the **Team** page to view all instructors
2. Click on team members to view their profiles
3. Note their names and specialties
4. In your inquiry, mention which team members you'd prefer to work with

### Step 7: Submit and Confirm

1. Review your information one more time
2. Accept our privacy policy
3. Click **"Send Inquiry"**
4. You'll see a confirmation message
5. A team member will contact you within 1-2 business days

## What Happens Next

### Initial Contact
Our team will:
- Confirm receipt of your inquiry
- Review your workshop selection and team needs
- Check availability for your preferred dates
- Suggest optimal workshop combinations

### Date Confirmation
We'll:
- Propose specific dates based on your availability
- Confirm the instructor and delivery format
- Discuss any customization options
- Provide a quote if applicable

### Booking Confirmation
Once dates are agreed:
- You'll receive a formal booking confirmation
- Payment details (if applicable) will be provided
- Pre-workshop materials will be sent
- Logistics information (location, time, what to bring)

### Before the Workshop
You'll receive:
- **Pre-workshop Setup Guide** - Software/tools to install
- **Learning Materials** - Background reading or preparation
- **Logistics Details** - Date, time, location, parking info
- **Instructor Bio** - Information about your instructor

### During the Workshop
- Morning/afternoon sessions typically 3 hours each
- Mix of theory and hands-on practice
- Live examples and code walkthroughs
- Q&A and problem-solving
- Breaks as needed

### After the Workshop
You'll get:
- **Certificate of Completion** - For participants
- **Learning Materials** - Slides, code samples, resources
- **Ongoing Support** - Email access to your instructor for questions

## Special Requests

### Customization
If our standard workshops don't quite fit:
- Mention custom requirements in your inquiry
- We can tailor content to your specific needs
- Pricing and dates will be discussed separately

### Multiple Teams
If you want workshops for different groups:
- Contact us to arrange batch scheduling
- We can coordinate logistics across teams
- Potential discounts for large group bookings

### Remote Delivery
All workshops can be delivered:
- In-person at your location (UK-wide)
- At our offices
- Hybrid (mix of remote and in-person)
- Fully remote (with setup for screen sharing)

## Cancellation & Rescheduling

### Reschedule Your Workshop
If you need to change dates:
1. Contact us as soon as possible
2. We'll work to find alternative dates
3. Some date changes are free; others may incur costs
4. Confirm new dates before the original workshop date

### Cancellation Policy
- Cancel more than 30 days in advance for full refund
- 30-14 days before: 50% refund
- Less than 14 days: No refund (but can reschedule)
- Contact us to discuss any exceptions

## FAQ - Workshop Booking

**Q: How far in advance should we book?**
A: We recommend 4-6 weeks, but can often accommodate shorter timelines. Contact us to check availability.

**Q: Can we customize a workshop for our team's specific needs?**
A: Yes! We offer bespoke customization. Mention this in your inquiry and we'll discuss options.

**Q: Do we need to have previous coding experience?**
A: It depends on the workshop. Check the difficulty level and prerequisites on the workshop page.

**Q: Can workshops be delivered remotely?**
A: Yes, all our workshops can be delivered online. Specify this in your booking form.

**Q: What if our team needs workshops on specific topics not listed?**
A: We can create custom workshops. Describe your needs in your inquiry and we'll suggest options.

**Q: How many people can attend one workshop?**
A: Standard workshops accommodate up to 4 people per session. Larger groups can book multiple sessions.

**Q: Are there discounts for multiple workshops or larger teams?**
A: Yes, we offer package deals. Discuss your volume needs when you inquire.

**Q: What software do we need before the workshop?**
A: That depends on the workshop. We'll send setup instructions once your booking is confirmed.

**Q: Can we get a certificate of completion?**
A: Yes, all participants receive a DreamLab certificate upon completion.

**Q: What if we need to cancel?**
A: See our cancellation policy above. Contact us to discuss your situation.

**Q: How do we pay for the workshop?**
A: We'll provide payment details after your booking is confirmed. We accept bank transfer and major credit cards.
