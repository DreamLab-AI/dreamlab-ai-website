# Chapter 5: The Broader Landscape: Models, Tools, and the Future of AI Coding

AI coding agents do not exist in isolation. They are built on rapidly evolving foundation models, compete within a rich tool ecosystem, and are shaped by broader trends in AI capabilities. Understanding this landscape helps you make informed decisions about which tools and models to use, and prepares you for what comes next.

This chapter explores:

*   [Agentic Tools vs. Chat Models](./05_a_codex_vs_general_gpt_models.md): The fundamental difference between tools that act in your environment versus models that generate text about code.
*   [Understanding Model & Tool Choices](./05_b_understanding_model_choices.md): Current models from Anthropic and OpenAI, their strengths, and how to choose between them.
*   [The Evolving Toolkit](./05_c_the_evolving_toolkit.md): Emerging capabilities, MCP adoption, and where the field is heading.

This broader perspective helps in making informed decisions about which tools and models to use for different scenarios and in anticipating future advancements in AI-assisted software engineering.
