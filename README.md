### Why Nostr?

- **No email required** â€” authenticate with cryptographic keys
- **You own your identity** â€” portable across any Nostr client
- **End-to-end encryption** â€” private messages stay private
- **No central authority** â€” distributed across independent relays

### Supported NIPs

| NIP | Feature |
|-----|---------|
